Helen Wang

Engineering, Design, Aviation, Robotics

Hello, I am Helen! - A Mechanical Engineer with a fervor for aviation, product design, eletric vehicle and robotics. 

After earning my Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Vermont, I started a new academic journey as a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University. 

Currently, I am a graduate student researcher at Airlab, where I'm conducting path planning research in VTOL and aerial robotics. I am also a part-time Fablab student worker at Carnegie Mellon's largest makerspace- TechSpark, where I practice and improve my prototyping skills and help students complete their projects. 

My professional journey has been enriched through my co-op role at Husky Injection Molding Systems and, more recently, through my internship at the eVTOL aerospace company BETA Technologies. 

Throughout my career and academic journey, I've developed my skills in a good combination of hardware design and software architecture projects.

Check out my project portfolio and see all the cool projects I've been working on!
