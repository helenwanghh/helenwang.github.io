Designing, Building, Always Progressing

Hello, I am Helen! - A Mechanical Engineer with passions for aviation, product design, eletric vehicle and robotics. 

After earning my Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Vermont, I started a new academic journey as a graduate student at Carnegie Mellon University. 

Currently, I am a graduate student researcher at Airlab, where I'm conducting path planning research in VTOL and aerial robotics. I am also a part-time Fablab student worker at Carnegie Mellon's largest makerspace- TechSpark, where I practice and improve my prototyping skills and help students complete their projects. Outside of class, I am a driver-interface team member of Carnegie Mellon Racing, where I work with the team to design and build competitive electric vehicle.

My professional journey has been enriched through my co-op role at Husky Injection Molding Systems and, more recently, through my internship at the eVTOL aerospace company BETA Technologies. 

Throughout my career and academic journey, I've developed my skills in product design and prototype projects, which blend with both hardware and software techniques. 

Check out my project portfolio and see all the cool projects I've been working on!
