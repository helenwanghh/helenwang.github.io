Designing, Building, Always Progressing

Hello, I am Helen! - A Mechanical Engineer with passions for product design, robotics, electric vehicle and aviation.

I'm graduating from Carnegie Mellon University in May 2025
with a Master of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering
 and concentration in robotics and mechanical design.

Currently, I am a Mechanical graduate student researcher at Airlab, where I work on electromechanical hardware systems for the aerial and ground robots.

I am also an Mechanical Design Engineer intern at Tesla Cell Manufacturing Engineering. I'm part of the cell mixing equipment team working on developing cathode recycle production line. 

Carnegie Mellon's largest makerspace- TechSpark, where I practice and improve my prototyping skills and help students complete their projects.

Outside of class, I am a powertrain team engineer at
Carnegie Mellon Racing, where I lead the cooling system design and build for a competitive electric vehicle.

My professional journey has been enriched through
work experience at Tesla (internship),
BETA Technologies (internship),
Husky Injection Molding Systems (co-op).

Throughout my career and academic journey, I've developed my skills in product design, fabrication, and testing.

Check out my project portfolio and see all the cool projects I've been working on!
