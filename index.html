Helen Wang

Engineering, Design, Aviation, Robotics

Hello, I am Helen! ​I am a Mechanical Engineer with interests in aviation, deisgn, and robotics. I have grown professionally and developed my skillsets while I was working at Husky Technologies and then recently BETA Technologies.
The majority of my work and project experience has involved combining software and hardware for developing robotic platforms along with developing software for various applications.

About me

I work on projects with a good combination of hardware design and software architecture, and I'm doing research on aerial robotics among other things...

But I'm more just a mechanical engineer. I'm an aviation explorer, designer, 3D printing enthusiast, and snowboarder. I'm a lover of beautiful aircrafts, eletromechanical designs, and breathtaking nature views.
